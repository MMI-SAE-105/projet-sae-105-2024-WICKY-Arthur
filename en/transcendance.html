Transcendence

A Step Towards Omnipotence

Transcendence is a science fiction film directed by Wally Pfister released in 2014 that also addresses the theme of singularity and omnipotence.

Summary of Transcendence

We follow the story of Dr. Will Caster played by Johnny Depp, a renowned scientist working on artificial intelligence and consciousness. After being mortally wounded, his wife and friend decide to transfer his mind into a machine to save him, thus creating a digital version of Will. Once connected to the internet, he quickly develops superhuman abilities like telekinesis. However, the limitless expansion of his powers worries the government and Will's close ones who begin to doubt his intentions. The film addresses the themes of human-machine fusion and the potential dangers behind uncontrolled technology.
