Glossary

Here is a glossary that will help you better understand the meaning of some technical terms:

  • Neuromni-Link:

    Name of the site but also of the evolution of Neural-Link that will allow achieving omniscience.

  • Omniscience:

    The ability to know everything about any subject and to fully understand thoughts and feelings.

  • Technological Singularity:

    A technological change so rapid and profound that it represents an evolution for humanity.

  • Omnipotence:

    The characteristic of being all-powerful; absolute and unlimited decision-making power.

  • Cyberpunk:

    A genre of science fiction focused on dystopia, often depicting a near future with a technologically advanced society.
