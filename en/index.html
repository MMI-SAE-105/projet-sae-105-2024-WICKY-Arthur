Neuromni-Link

In a distant future, the entire world is equipped with an advanced and perfected version of Neuralink. This Neuromni-Link is nanoscopic in size and implanted at birth. In this way, humans are all connected to the same freely accessible internet network, reaching a certain level of omniscience.

The Mobile Phone

The mobile phone is an essential element of our society, but the Neuromni-Link will allow technology to be manipulated through the mind, without any device, making the smartphone obsolete.

Accelerando

Accelerando is a science fiction novel written by Charles Stross. The novel addresses the theme of singularity, a moment when technology progresses to such an extent that it would allow humans to surpass the limits of their own mind and body.

Transcendence

Transcendence is a science fiction film directed by Wally Pfister and released in 2014. It follows the story of a scientist whose mind is placed in the first supercomputer in history. He can then control all networks connected to the internet, becoming almost omnipotent.

Wally Pfister

Wally Pfister is an American director and cinematographer. He directed the film "Transcendence" but is known for his work as a cinematographer on Christopher Nolan's films, for which he has received nominations and awards.

Trinity

Trinity is a rap album associated with the Cyberpunk genre by its sounds but also its story. The album questions the concepts of reality and virtuality through the complex romantic relationship the artist has with a young woman named Trinity.
