Demon Seed

1977

Science-fiction, Horror

By Donald Cammell
With Julie Christie, Fritz Weaver, Gerrit Graham

Feature film by Donald Cammell - 1h34min - released in 1977

Genres : Science-fiction, Horreur

Origin Country : United-States

Synopsis : Alex Harris is a brilliant professor of cybernetics. He has created a computer with an artificial brain that took him many years to build. Its name is Proteus IV, and it talks just like a human. Its logical and reflexive abilities are developing exponentially, but very soon the computer's emerging curiosity reveals a weird desire to study humans.
