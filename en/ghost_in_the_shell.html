Ghost in the Shell

1995

Science-fiction, action, crime

By Mamoru Oshii
With Atsuko Tanaka, Aiko Ōtsuka, Iemasa Kayumi

Animated movie by Mamoru Oshii - 1h22min - released in 1995

Genres : Science-fiction, Action, Crime

Origin Country : Japan

Synopsis : In 2029, in a futuristic Japan governed by the Internet, Major Motoko Kusunagi, an ultra-sophisticated female cyborg, is left to wonder. In spite of herself, she is part of a cyber-police force with virtually unlimited resources to fight computer crime. As her section tracks down the 'Puppet Master', a legendary and mysterious hacker, she gives herself the task of analyzing his ghost (an element of consciousness that can be compared to the soul) in the hope of finding answers to her own existential questions.
