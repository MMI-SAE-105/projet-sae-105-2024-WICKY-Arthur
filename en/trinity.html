Trinity

When Reality and Virtuality Blur

Trinity is a French rap album released in 2020 that belongs to the Cyberpunk genre, where the concepts of reality and virtuality are blurred.

Introduction to Laylow

Jérémy Larroux, known as Laylow, is a young artist from Toulouse born on March 12, 1993. He is a French rapper and singer whose music stands out for its futuristic dimension in terms of voice and autotune usage. He is also a director and has directed his own music videos as well as those of other French rappers like Nekfeu.

Analysis of Trinity

In this album, the artist faces a complex romantic relationship with a young woman named Trinity. Through the album's story and various interludes, it becomes clear that his relationship with Trinity does not exist, as Trinity is not a woman but an emotion-stimulating software that amplifies everything the artist feels artificially. This album questions the concepts of reality and virtuality through the Trinity software, which connects these two concepts.
